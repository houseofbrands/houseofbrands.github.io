23 Years of Excellence in the Watch Industry

With 23 years of experience in the watch industry and still counting, Rajan Mehta has built an extensive expertise in design, manufacturing, and brand development. His journey in the world of watches has been shaped by working with some of the most renowned brands globally, gaining deep insights into trends, technology, and consumer preferences.

A Legacy of Learning & Growth

Rajan's career in watches began with working for some of the most established brands in the industry, where he honed his skills in design, sourcing, manufacturing, and retail strategies. Over a span of 12 years, he contributed to and learned from leading watch brands, including:

  • Maxima – Understanding mass-market affordability while maintaining quality.
  • Timex – Learning international watchmaking standards and timeless craftsmanship.
  • Danish Design – Exploring Scandinavian minimalism and premium aesthetics.
  • Jacob Jensen – Mastering modern, high-design timepieces with global appeal.
  • Lee Cooper – Developing trend-driven watches with a strong fashion influence.
  • Kappa – Combining sportiness with style for an active lifestyle audience.

These experiences laid the foundation for Rajan's deep expertise in the watch industry, allowing him to grasp the intricacies of product development, supply chain management, and market positioning.

Building a Watch Manufacturing Powerhouse

In the last 11 years, Rajan took his expertise further by establishing his own watch manufacturing business in India & China. Today, he stands as a leading OEM partner, producing high-quality watches for multiple prestigious brands.

Currently, he manufactures watches for all major Myntra private-label brands, including:

  • Roadster – Rugged, casual timepieces designed for everyday wear.
  • DressBerry – Elegant, feminine watches for modern women.
  • Mast & Harbour – Classic and versatile designs for fashion-conscious consumers.
  • Wrogn – Youth-centric, edgy watches that make a bold statement.
  • Killer – Stylish and urban timepieces for trendsetters.
  • Provogue – Premium watches with a touch of sophistication.
  • Metronaut – Sleek and performance-driven watches for an active lifestyle.
  • House of Pataudi – Heritage-inspired designs blending tradition with modernity.

Innovation & Scale in Watch Manufacturing

  • Developed Over 800 Unique Watch Models – Successfully designed and developed more than 800 different watch models in India.
  • Selling on India's Top E-commerce Platforms – Watch collections available on major platforms like Myntra, Flipkart, Noon, Reliance, and FirstCry.
  • Helping Brands Grow – Helping multiple Indian brands launch and scale their watch collections with superior design and quality.

A Leader in the Watch Industry

From working with global watch brands to leading large-scale watch manufacturing, Rajan Mehta has played a significant role in shaping the watch market in India. With 23 years of expertise and counting, he continues to set new standards in watchmaking, design, and business growth.
