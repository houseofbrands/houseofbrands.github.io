About Me – Rajan Mehta, A Visionary in Fashion & Retail

Rajan Mehta is a seasoned entrepreneur and industry leader with over two decades of experience in the watch and fashion accessories business. With a sharp eye for trends and a passion for innovation, he has played a pivotal role in shaping the Indian fashion and retail landscape.

Career & Achievements

Rajan began his journey in the watch industry 22 years ago and quickly established himself as a trusted OEM partner for numerous prestigious watch brands. Over the years, he expanded his expertise into Watches, Eyewear, Lingerie, and Leather Accessories, ensuring his brands remain at the forefront of fashion and functionality.

As a Partner at Myntra, Rajan leads the design and development of watches and sunglasses for Myntra’s Private Label, bringing high-quality, stylish products to one of India's largest fashion marketplaces. Additionally, as the Director of House of Brands, he has successfully built a high-turnover company with a strong market presence. Today, House of Brands operates across three global offices, reflecting its growing international footprint.

His business acumen extends to wholesale and retail, with House of Brands Wholesale catering to large-scale buyers, and a dedicated retail store offering watches, sunglasses, apparel, and shoes. His collaborations with major platforms like Noon, Myntra, Flipkart, Reliance, and FirstCry underscore his deep industry connections and market expertise.

With a strong presence in the industry, Rajan has successfully sold to over 10 million customers till date—and still counting. His commitment to quality, affordability, and trend-driven designs has made his brands a household name.

Leadership & Operations

At House of Brands, Rajan oversees all leading departments, including Manufacturing, Marketing & E-commerce, and Logistics, ensuring smooth operations and business growth. He leads a team of over 50 professionals, driving innovation, efficiency, and customer satisfaction.

Education & Vision

Rajan holds a Master’s degree from IIM Calcutta, which has played a crucial role in shaping his strategic and leadership skills. As a fashion enthusiast and trendsetter, he continues to push boundaries, setting new standards in the Indian retail and fashion industry while building brands that resonate with modern consumers.
